# RHM brand architecture

## RHM Studios

Use **RHM Studios** for all media production:

- full devotional videos
- audio masters
- social clips and captions
- thumbnails
- B-roll and scripture graphics used in video
- StreamYard-ready export packages

The supplied RHM logo is stored at `assets/rhm-logo.png`. Video renders should support a user-controlled brand bug with position, size, opacity, safe-area padding, and optional intro/outro placement. The logo must never be permanently applied to source media; apply it only to previews and approved exports.

## RHM Publications

Use **RHM Publications** for all written and publishing work:

- approved devotionals and prayers
- scripture indexes
- ebooks and collections
- PDF, EPUB, and print-ready exports
- covers and interior title pages

Publications may use the same RHM logo with the words “RHM Publications.” Cover placement and interior branding require user approval before export.
